The postcard generator has been created by the fifth cohort of the 23 Code Street's web development foundation course.

This is a great and funny tool designed to create a fun, interactive postcard, that you can send to friends, colleagues, family etc.

Just choose a picture of your choice, or upload one of your own and start the fun with different styling, colours and text.
